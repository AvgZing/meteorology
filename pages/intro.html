Definitions

In this first part, you will learn what the words "temperature", "pressure", "wind", "relative humidity", "clouds", and "precipitation" mean.

Temperature

How hot or cold an object or substance is. Temperature is usually measured with a temperature.

Pressure

The amount of force that is applied on a certain area.

Wind

The natural movement of the air in a certain direction.

Relative Humidity

How much water vapor is in the air compared to how much there could be.
